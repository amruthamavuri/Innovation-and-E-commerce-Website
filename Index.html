What is Angel Investment?

Angel investment involves affluent individuals providing capital to start-ups in exchange for ownership equity or convertible debt. These investors believe in the innovative potential of early-stage companies and offer not just financial support but also mentorship and guidance.

If you are brimming with innovative ideas and seek funding, V on C invites you to connect with potential angel investors who can help bring your vision to life.
